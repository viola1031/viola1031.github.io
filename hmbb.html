Pai Daxing, the character in the American cartoon "SpongeBob SquarePants" and its derivative works, is also a good friend of SpongeBob SquarePants. He is a pink starfish with a rough voice, a simple mind, and well-developed limbs. He often causes trouble for everyone and drools non-stop when he is sleeping or in a daze.

SpongeBob SquarePants is a square-shaped yellow sponge that lives in a pineapple on Bikini Beach. His pet is a sea snail called "cat~cat~". SpongeBob likes to catch jellyfish, and his occupation is Krab King The number one chef. Patrick and Sandy are both his friends.
